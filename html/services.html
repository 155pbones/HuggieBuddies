Our Services

At HuggieBuddies, we strive to provide your furkid with the best possible service!

What We Provide

Free to roam around the house

Huggiebuddies ensures that your precious furkid is happy as we provide it with the freedom to roam anywhere they want in the house, even the bedroom. This is to ensure that your furkid is happy and feels at home, being able to traverse anywehre in the house and live as just like a family member! Your furkid's happiness and comfort is our upmost priority!

Unendning Fun

Huggiebuddies ensures that your precious furkid gets the most attention and time as our team will play and entertain them with all sorts of toys. Apart from that, if there are multiple boardings, your furkid can play with the other furkids and make new friends. This will help your furkid to develop much needed social skills and have a great fun time!

Sleep anywhere

Huggiebuddies ensures that your precious furkids have the best sleep in town because we allow them to sleep anywere, even with us, on our beds. This is maximum comfort for your precious furkids and they can recieve the greatest sleep quality and nights of their lives! Sleep is vital for any living being and your precious furkids sure do need it!

At least 2 walks a day

Huggiebuddies ensures that your precious furkid gets enough sunlight as we walk him/her at least 2 times a day. This helps to improve your furkid's fitness! From our experience, most of the furkids we have boarded love taking long and exciting walks outside.
